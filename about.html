About Spring Lake Elementary

School Background

Spring Lake Elementary was established in 1966 and has served the community with an outstanding education for many years. The school is located in Altamonte Springs, Florida, which is about 15 miles outside of Orlando, Florida. Spring Lake has been a Five Star School for the past 5 years. Our Principal, Mr. Alex Agosto and Assistant Principal, Ms. Kimberly Lochrane are committed to setting a standard of excellence. The current focus for Spring Lake Elementary is the revitalization of classroom instruction utilizing current technology and research to promote positive collaboration, enhance learning and create a respectful climate that will encourage students to reach their learning potential.

Our Technology

Teachers and students use computers and technology as a part of their daily curriculum. Each classroom has an average of three computers and the school has three labs with 28 computers in each for student use and one mini lab with 15 computers. Our SuccessMaker Labs allow students in Grades 1-5 to practice math and reading skills aligned with the Next Generation Sunshine State Standards for their grade level. The production lab is used by teachers when incorporating technology such as: Webquests, digital storytelling, Excel spreadsheets, PowerPoint projects, and virtual field trips. We have Tungsten E2 Hand-held computers, BrightLinks, ActivBoard, AlphaSmart NEOs, 4 wireless laptop computer labs and many other technological tools.

Registration

a. Owned Residence: Warrant Deed OR Homestead Exemption, AND a copy of a current utility bill or initial order for service; AND one of the following current documents supporting stated address: auto registration, driver's license, or voter's registration as evidence that parent(s) own and live at the residence.

b. Rented or leased residence: Current lease OR rental agreement OR a notarized letter from the landlord, AND a copy of a current utility bill OR initial order for service; AND one of the following current documents supporting stated address: auto registration, driver's license, or voter's registration as evidence that parent(s) live at the residence.

c. Non-primary owner or renter: notarized statement from primary resident indicating residence status in addition TWO of the following current documents: utility bill, auto registration, driver's license, or voter's registration as proof that parent(s) own and live at the residence.

d. Divorced Parents: a certified copy of the final judgment of divorce, a court custody order, a court guardianship order, or other such document establishing the right of custody.

e. Separated Parents: a notarized statement that the child actually lives in the home of the parent designated in the child's school records as the residential parent.

Proof of Residency

Birth Certificate Social Security Card Report Card - for grade verification if applicable HRS 680 Florida certificate of Immunization Certificate showing a school- entry Health Examination performed within one year prior to enrollment (dated and signed by a health professional.
